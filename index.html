Our Story

Order Order opened in June 2015 and is located in the Riverdale section of the Bronx. Since then, award winning Head Chef and owner Naomi Chris has been serving electrifying renditions of old classics in her native borough.
Order Order gives a new spin on the classic dinner. And old time flavore with a new hip, family friendly flare. It's interior is welcoming with a bright exuberant atmosphere and upbeat and comforting music.

Head Chef Naomi Chris
